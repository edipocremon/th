TerraHidro User Manual

4 How to Cite

When referencing this manual, use the APA and BibTeX entries below.
When citing the TerraHidro software/system itself (methods and implementation), please cite the conference paper listed in the second set.

Cite the Manual

TerraHidro Team. (2025). TerraHidro Manual (Version 5.2.0) [Bookdown manual]. INPE. http://www.dpi.inpe.br/terrahidro/doku.php

Optional (with access date):
TerraHidro Team. (2025). TerraHidro Manual (Version 5.2.0) [Bookdown manual]. INPE. http://www.dpi.inpe.br/terrahidro/doku.php (accessed Month Day, Year)

Cite the Manual (BibTeX)

@manual{TerraHidroManual2025,
  title        = {TerraHidro Manual},
  author       = {{TerraHidro Team}},
  organization = {INPE},
  year         = {2025},
  edition      = {Version 5.2.0},
  url          = {http://www.dpi.inpe.br/terrahidro/doku.php},
  note         = {Bookdown manual}
}

Cite the TerraHidro System (APA)

Rosim, S., Oliveira, J. R. F., Jardim, A. C., Namikawa, L. M., & Rennó, C. D. (2013). TerraHidro: A distributed hydrology modelling system with high quality drainage extraction. In Proceedings of the 5th International Conference on Advanced Geographic Information Systems, Applications, and Services (pp. 161–167). Nic e, France. ISBN 978-1-61208-251-6. Available at http://www.thinkmind.org/index.php?view=article&articleid=geoprocessing_2013_7_20_30125
